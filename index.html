Services & Pricing

Pre‑Wedding Photography

Capture the excitement of your engagement with intimate sessions before your big day.

Typical price: 500–2,000 AED for 1–3 hour sessions.

Post‑Wedding Sessions

Enjoy relaxed portraits after the wedding in your favourite locations.

Typical price: 1,500–5,000 AED depending on session length and deliverables.

Wedding & Event Coverage

Comprehensive photography & videography for weddings, corporate gatherings and celebrations.

Packages usually range from 1,500–8,000 AED, with hourly rates around 300–500 AED for photography and 350–600 AED for videography.

Model Portfolio Sessions

Professional fashion shoots to build your modelling portfolio with guidance and styling.

Typical packages start from 2,000–5,000 AED; per‑photo rates vary between 409–680 AED.

Videography

Dynamic video production for events, promotions and social media.

Hourly videography rates are about 350–600 AED; half‑day coverage ranges from 1,200–1,800 AED.

Headshots & Portraits

Crisp headshots and portraits for professional profiles and personal branding.

Budget sessions start around 250–400 AED; creative portrait shoots range between 500–2,000 AED.

Baby & Newborn Shoots

Gentle, patient sessions to capture your little one’s earliest moments.

Packages range from 800–1,400 AED for 15–40 edited photos.

Product Photography

High‑quality images that highlight the details of your products for e‑commerce and marketing.

Per‑photo pricing varies from 409–680 AED, with discounted rates for larger batches.

Automobile Shoots

Sleek automotive imagery showcasing vehicles in stunning environments.

Typical sessions start around 500–1,000 AED.

About Me

I’m Kamesh, a photographer and videographer with several years of experience capturing the essence of life through my lens. I specialise in fashion portfolios, family moments, events, real estate and product photography. My aim is to blend creativity with technical expertise to produce visuals that tell compelling stories. I believe in working closely with every client to understand their unique vision and goals. Whether it’s a vibrant fashion shoot, a heartfelt family gathering or showcasing a stunning property, my mission is to create images and videos that resonate and leave a lasting impression. Let’s work together to bring your vision to life!
